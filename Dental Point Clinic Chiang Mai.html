A warm welcome

New in town, or just visiting? You're in good hands.

Far from home, a toothache can feel daunting. At Dental Point, English-speaking care isn't an afterthought — it's how we greet every international patient who walks in from the Old City.

We take the time to listen, explain each step in plain English, and talk you through what care you need and what it costs — before anything begins. No surprises, no language barrier, just calm and considered dentistry a short stroll from the temples and moat.
