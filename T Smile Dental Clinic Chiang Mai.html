Welcome, in English

Far from home, but cared for close by.

Choosing a dentist in a new country can feel daunting. Here, beneath Doi Suthep, you are met in plain English by a team that takes the time to explain every step — gently, clearly, and without rush.

From a quick routine clean to a full course of orthodontic treatment, international patients and long-stay visitors find unhurried, attentive care that feels reassuringly familiar.

  • Everything explained in English — your questions answered before anything begins.
  • Open every day until 8pm — easy to fit around travel and work.
  • A calm setting on Suthep Road — a few minutes from the old city and the mountain.
