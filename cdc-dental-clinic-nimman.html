A clinic that speaks your language

New in town? You're in good hands.

Finding a dentist abroad can feel daunting — the words, the prices, the unknowns. Here in Nimman, that worry disappears at the door.

CDC Dental Clinic sits right in Nimmanhaemin, the café-lined, Wi-Fi-friendly neighbourhood that so many remote workers and visitors call home. We're used to international patients, so every consultation, every treatment plan and every cost is explained clearly in English — no guesswork, no language barrier.

Whether you're here for a quick clean between flights or settling in for a longer stay, you'll find an unhurried, modern clinic where you can relax like you would at your favourite café.
