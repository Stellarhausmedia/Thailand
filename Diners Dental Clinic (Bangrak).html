Visitor Information

New in Bangkok? You're in good hands here.

Far from home, choosing a dentist should feel easy. At Diners Dental we look after a steady stream of expats, travellers and international residents, and every step — from your first hello to your treatment plan and the bill — is explained clearly in English.

No jargon, no surprises. Just calm, attentive care in a quiet studio a short stroll from Charoen Krung's galleries, riverside hotels and creative spaces.
