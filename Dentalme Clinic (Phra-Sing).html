You're Welcome Here

Far from home, but perfectly understood.

Choosing a dentist in a new country shouldn't feel daunting. At Dentalme, English isn't an afterthought — it's how we greet you, explain every option, and confirm your appointment.

From the first WhatsApp message to your treatment plan, you'll always know exactly what's happening and why. It's modern dental care with the unhurried warmth of Chiang Mai's craft quarter.

  • Book & chat in English over WhatsApp — ask questions, share concerns, and arrange your visit in your own words.
  • An English-language treatment menu so prices and procedures are clear before you ever sit in the chair.
  • Right in the historic Old City — easy to reach whether you're visiting Chiang Mai or settling in long-term.
