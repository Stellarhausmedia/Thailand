The English-speaking part

No translating.
Just teeth.

Visitors tell us the same thing on the way out — “they spoke English with me” — and a lot of them come back every Bangkok trip.

Sitting in a dentist’s chair is strange enough without a language gap. So here, you’ll be understood in plain English from the moment you say hello — what’s happening, what it costs, and what comes next, with nothing lost in translation.

We’re a small, On Nut clinic that’s grown used to looking after travellers, remote workers and the café-and-laptop crowd around Sukhumvit 77/1. Drop in for a check, a fix or a whole new smile — and pick up right where you left off next time you’re in town.
