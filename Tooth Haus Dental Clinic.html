The English welcome

No translation
needed.

Moving to a new country is enough to navigate — finding a dentist you trust shouldn't add to it. At Tooth Haus, every consultation, diagnosis and treatment plan is explained in clear, friendly English, so you always know exactly what's happening and why.

Our patients come from across Bangkok and far beyond. In fact, one of them flies in from the United States just to sit in our chair — proof that the right care is worth the distance. Whether you're an expat settling into Sathorn or a visitor passing through, you'll be looked after like a regular from your very first appointment.
