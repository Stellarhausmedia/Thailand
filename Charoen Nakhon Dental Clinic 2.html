Welcome

A familiar face at the river’s landmark

Visiting Bangkok or living here long-term, dental care shouldn’t feel like a translation exercise. At ICONSIAM you’ll find a clinic where everything — your questions, your treatment plan, your follow-up — happens in clear, unhurried English.

Care that travels with you

Our patients include Thai-American families and international visitors who pass through ICONSIAM again and again — and trust the same chair each time. That continuity is the whole point: you’re recognised, your history is on file, and the conversation picks up right where it left off.

Whether you’re here for a single visit or you call Bangkok home, you’ll be guided gently through every option before anything begins.
