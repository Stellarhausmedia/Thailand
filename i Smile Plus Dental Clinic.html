Travellers find their way to us from all over the world.

Far from home and need a dentist you can talk to? You're in good company. Visitors making their way through Chiang Mai — from Australia to Germany and far beyond — have called us one of the friendliest clinics in the city.

Every consultation, every step of your treatment, every question about cost and care is handled in clear, comfortable English. No translation worries, no guesswork — just calm, attentive dentistry.

Whether you're here for a week or living the Chiang Mai life, you'll be met with the same warm welcome and the same gentle hands.
