Treatments

A full range of care, gently delivered.

From routine check-ups to complete treatment plans — thoughtful, modern dentistry for the whole family, all under one riverside roof.

01

General Dentistry

Check-ups, professional cleaning, scaling and polishing to keep your smile healthy, bright and comfortable all year round.

02

Fillings & Restoration

Tooth-coloured fillings and restorations that blend in naturally, repairing decay and small damage so teeth feel whole again.

03

Root Canal Care

Comfortable, careful treatment to save a painful or infected tooth — relieving discomfort while keeping your natural smile intact.

04

Cosmetic & Whitening

Brightening and aesthetic treatments to refresh your smile — subtle, natural results designed around the look you're hoping for.

05

Crowns & Bridges

Strong, lifelike crowns and bridges to rebuild and replace teeth — restoring both the function and the natural feel of your bite.

06

Family & Preventive

Gentle care for every age, with friendly guidance on keeping teeth strong between visits — comfortable for nervous patients too.
